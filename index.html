OUR SERVICES

Manufacturing

Establishing or expanding manufacturing operations in Thailand and the APAC region takes more than a good product — it takes the right location, the right partners, and a clear read on incentives and infrastructure. We help clients find and validate that path.

Go-to-Market Manufacturing Economic Development

Site Selection

We identify and evaluate candidate sites against your operational requirements — labor availability, utilities, zoning, and proximity to suppliers and logistics — so you shortlist with confidence rather than guesswork.

Location Analysis

We compare regions and industrial estates across Thailand and APAC on cost, workforce, infrastructure, and BOI/FDI incentives — giving you a clear, data-backed basis for where to locate your operation.

Contract Manufacturing

Where building your own facility isn't the right first step, we identify and vet co-packing and co-production partners — qualified manufacturing partners who can produce to your specification without the capital outlay.
